    • about me

    Hello! My name is Jenny Tiger and I am a 4th year student pursuing a Master's degree in Interaction Technology and Design at Umeå University.

    Through my education I have become adept in many programming languages and techniques such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, C#, and Python. Other skills and experiences I have gathered throughout the years are React, ASP.NET, SQL and WordPress. I also very familiar with some mathematical and statistical programs such as Rstudio and MatLab.

    Even if I have many different development experiences, I will always love and be most fond of the design process and front-end development - including every step from Discover to Design. I am well adept in multiple design tools such as Figma and Photoshop. Design thinking? Yeah we learn that too in my education!

    I am a problem solver at heart and have always enjoyed the journey from a problem or idea to a finished product or solution. I like every step of the way and if there is a way to connect with a costumer, I’m up for it.

    manifesto

    remain a student

    Stay updated to new trends and techniques while always keeping an open mind to learn something new.

    do your research

    Don't just go by your own preferences. Listen to users and stakeholders and let real situations determine what is best.

    keep it simple

    Less is more. It is difficult to create a simple and easy design but all work on the way there is worth it. The best user interface is the one you don't notice!

    why design?

    Honestly, I ended up in this field by chance. The reason why I stayed and have continued to pursue this degree is because it's a perfect match for me. I love everything that has to do with design and figuration - anything creative really.



    During my time after graduating gymnasium (upper secondary school) I learnt a lot by just figuring out who I am and what I like to do. I worked full-time in costumer support, further developing my understanding for people and individuals diverse perceptions and needs. Today I can use my creative side together with the experiences from my rogue year to build and design solutions that are made for people. I am now on a journey of learning more skills within this field and I hope to keep learning far into my working life.



    If I'm not designing, cursing over merge conflicts or taking a new course at uni, then you'll find me in the gym, in the kitchen cooking or baking, or going on an adventure with by boyfriend and dog.

    this doesn't have to be the end, let's connect!
